PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Universe Sim ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website at mateusbrta.github.io (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Universe Sim does not collect, store or transmit any personal information. The simulator runs entirely in your browser with no user accounts, no login system and no backend server operated by us.

The only data stored is in your own browser's localStorage, used solely to remember your preferred language and whether you have dismissed the tutorial popup. This data never leaves your device.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, please see Google's Privacy & Terms.

Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. Google AdSense may set third-party cookies for advertising purposes as described above. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

Universe Sim is a general-audience educational simulation suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
