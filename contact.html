What to Include

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Your browser name and version (e.g. Chrome 122, Firefox 124)
  • Your operating system
  • A description of what you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
  • Any error messages from the browser console (press F12 to open it)

Universe Sim is a hobby project maintained in spare time. We read every message and do our best to respond promptly, but please allow a few days for a reply.
